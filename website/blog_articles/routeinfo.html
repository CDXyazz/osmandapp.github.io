Routeinfo - full information about your trip.

October 23, 2019

After the route is built, you can press the "Details" button, which hides the additional graphs. In the 3.4.2 release, there are Road types, Surface, Smoothness, Steepness, Surface firmness (Tracktype), Winter, and ice roads available. The graphs are interactive and synchronized with one another and the route overview map.
