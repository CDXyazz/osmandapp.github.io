OsmAnd Online GPS Tracker 0.8

January 21, 2020

Hi!

It was a long period without updated of our Online Tracker.

We prepared new features for you!

Suggested list of contacts

At this release we add Suggested list of contacts. What is it? It's your last five contact which you shared your location. Now you can click one time for continue sharing your location for this contacts.

Time of sharing is your last choosing sharing time for contact.

When you start sharing, you can see in the "My location" screen your suggested screen and button "Back to OsmAnd".

Online Tracker Widget in OsmAnd

We added Online Tracker Widget to OsmAnd. For viewing the Widget you should switch on OsmAmd Tracke plugin at first. After that the Widget will be at ther right corner of the OsmAnd screen with signiture "Start".

For switch off it you can go to General menu-> Confugure screen(OsmAnd version 3.6).

The widget has few appearance. At first it is "Start". When you click to "Start" you go to Online OsmAnd Tracker, where you can choose any contacts or click to Suggested contacts.

When you start sharing of your location the Widget changes appearance.

The green smile means that your location sends now.

It's mean that everything is ok. The sharing hasn't ussiues.

The orange smile means that your location doesn't send. There are issues with your Internet or with GPS connection.

Yellow smile means has some problem with sharing: -not sattelites -not GSM connection

If you don't have internet connection long time. You buffer in OsmAnd Tracker fills saved points of your location. The Widget in OsmAnd changes it apperance. It starts to show time of the buffer filling. How long buffer start keap point of location.

When your Internet connection is restored. Points from buffer is sent. The Widget in OsmAnd change it appearance. Time is less, when it is zero. The Widget will be the green smile.

On OsmAnd you can see that the Widget has green smile. It's mean that everything is ok. The sharing hasn't ussiues.

Added "Back to OsmAnd" button

This button appears in "My location" screen, when we started sharing location. Now we have only one click for moving forward to OsmAnd.

Buffer Settings

We can set time for buffer. Now you can choose time to store points in the buffer. For choosing time you should go to Settings->Position->Buffer expiration time.

Units and formats Settings

We add settings for units and formats. Now you can choose your comfortable values for Telegram messages during sharing location. For changing its values you should go to Settings->Units & formats.
