OsmAnd Online GPS Tracker 0.8

January 21, 2020

Hi!

It was a long period without updated of our Online Tracker.

We prepared new features for you!

Suggested list of contacts

At this release, we add Suggested list of contacts. What is it? It's your last five contacts in which you shared your location. Now you can click one time to continue sharing your location to these contacts.

Time of sharing is your last choosing sharing time for contact.

When you start sharing, you can see in the "My location" screen your Suggested list and button "Back to OsmAnd".

Online Tracker Widget in OsmAnd

We added Online Tracker Widget to OsmAnd. For viewing the Widget you should switch on OsmAnd Tracker plugin at first. After that, the Widget will be at the right corner of the OsmAnd screen with the signature "Start".

To switch off it, you can go to the General menu-> Configure screen(OsmAnd version 3.6).

The Widget has few appearances. At first, it is "Start". When you click to "Start" you go to Online OsmAnd Tracker, where you can choose any contacts or click to Suggested contacts.

When you start sharing your location the Widget changes appearance.

The green smile "OK" means that your location sends now.

It's mean that everything is ok. The sharing hasn't issues.

The orange smile "OK" means that your location doesn't send. There are issues with your Internet or with GPS connection.

If you don't have an internet connection for a long time. Your buffer in OsmAnd Tracker saves points of your location. The Widget in OsmAnd changes its appearance. It starts to show the time of the buffer filling. How long the buffer starts to keep points of your location.

When your internet connection is restored. Points from the buffer are sent. The Widget in OsmAnd changes its appearance. Minutes in the Widget become less. When all points from the buffer send to Telegram, the Widget will be a green smile.

On OsmAnd you can see that the Widget has a green smile. It's mean that everything is ok. The sharing hasn't issues.

Added "Back to OsmAnd" button

This button appears in "My location" screen, when we started sharing location. Now we have only one click for moving forward to OsmAnd.

Buffer Settings

We can set time for buffer. Now you can choose time to keep saving points in the buffer. For choosing the time you should go to Settings->Position->Buffer expiration time.

Units and formats Settings

We added settings for units and formats. Now you can choose your comfortable values for Telegram messages during sharing location. For changing its values you should go to Settings->Units & formats.
