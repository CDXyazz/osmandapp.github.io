OsmAnd 3.5

October 30, 2019

It was a long period after the last release. All this time our team worked with the new version of OsmAnd. We proudly present some major improvements and new features and hope you will enjoy them:

Updated application and profile Settings.

We think that it is not very convenient to use default profiles for a very specific use-case. We give the opportunity our users to make your profile with all settings, icons and etc.

We modified general menu. Now you can choose and set any parameters for the navigation profile and switch on special plugins for each profile.

Now you can create your own navigation profiles based on default profiles but with your parameters. For this you should choose in general menu Settings -> Manage application parametres. In the new window, you should click "+Add".

We show an example of how to make your own profile. We choose Offroad type of moving by car. When you will use passes in forests, mountains and etc.

At first, you should choose a base profile. It is "Driving", but for this use-case, you can choose "Cycling" too. Next, we give the name of our profile - "Offroad" and choose Navigation type. It is "Bicycle".

After that, we choose icon and color for our Navigation profile. How you can see we prepared many icons for your future choices.

We click the button "SAVE" for saving our "Offroad" navigation profile.

After that, we go to the general menu, choose "Offroad" navigation profile. We set all our own parameters for the screen, map, navigation, and general settings. Of course, here we can switch on needed plugins.

Now we have our "Offroad" navigation profile in Panel of navigation. We can switch on or switch off our profile in "Application profiles" menu.

At the next article in our blog we will show you any example with navigation profiles. It will be Brouter and routing.xml for "Navigation type".

New map download dialog.

Now when you want to have a needed map you can choose it at the World map and OsmAnd suggests downloading a map.

Night theme fixes

Fixed few routing issues around the world

Updated basemap with more detailed road network

Fixed flooded areas around the world

Ski routing: added height profile and route complexity to the route details
