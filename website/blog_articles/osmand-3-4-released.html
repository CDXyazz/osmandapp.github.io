OsmAnd 3.4

June 27, 2019

We are glad to announce the release of version 3.4! We proudly present some major improvements and new features, and hope you will enjoy them:

- Application profiles: create your own profile for your needs, with a custom icon and color.

- Add settings for default and min/max speed for profiles

- Add a widget with current coordinates

- Add options to show the compass on the map with a radius ruler

- Improved map downloads in the background

- Fixed Wikipedia language selection

- Fixed compass button behavior during navigation

- Bug fixes

Please update OsmAnd and share your opinion on our Facebook, Twitter, and Reddit!
