OsmAnd 3.8

September 10, 2020

Great news for Android users!

We're happy to announce the new 3.8 OsmAnd release for Android devices.

Update your OsmAnd, the new version has become even more convenient for your trips!

  • Updated "Plan Route" mode
  • Improved bicycle nodes visibility
  • Context menu for tracks with basic info
  • Improved "Search" algorithms
  • Improved "Follow track" options in Navigation
  • Fixed issues with import/export of profile settings
  • What else is in this release?

    • Updated "Plan Route" mode

    We renamed "Measure distance" tool to "Plane a route". Updated "Plan a route" mode allows using different navigation types for each segment and attaches any track to the roads. What does it mean?

    Little exapmle. You want to have a route for your Enduro trip. The first part of your trip is road from your home to offroad part, second part is inpaved road, and third part is road of your retarning to home.

    Go to General menu -> Plane a route. Choose "Create new route", next click to Profile icon and choose Road Profile for first part of our trip. After that we add start and end point. The first segment was built.

    For second part of our trip we click to Profile icon and choose our Enduro navigation profile (Prefer unpaved roads) and "Next segment" (Only the next segment will be recalculated using selected profile) . After that we choose points for our offroad trip.

    For last part (returned to home) we choose Road profile and add end point of our trip. Click to "Done" button and save (and rename) our GPX file with our journey trip.

    Now, we click to our track, change Appearance of the track. Click "Directions" button, and start our trip using GPX track which we built in "Plane a route" tool.

    Improved bicycle nodes visibility

    Good news for our users with bicycle trips. Now we improve bicycle nodes visibility. First step for add viewing nodes, go to menu -> Configure profile (Cycling, for example) -> Configure map -> Routes. In "Routes" you should choose "Show node network cycle routes".

    Context menu for tracks with basic info

    At this release we improve context menu for tracks and add to opportunity to choose menu by clicking a track on the map.

    When you click the track you can see all infromation about the track: point, overwiew, altitude, speed.

    When you click to "Open Track" button, you see track menu where you can change apperance of the track, show on/off the track on the map, see graphs (overview, altitude, speed), join segments, split interval and analyse on the map.

    When you click to "Analyse on map" button, you can see needed information about altitude, speed, slope in each points of your track in graphs and on the map.

    When you click to "Open Track" button, you see track menu where you can change apperance of the track, show on/off the track on the map, see graphs (overview, altitude, speed), join segments, split interval and analyse on the map.

    Improved "Search" algorithms

    We fixed many bugs in our search algorithms. Now you can find places, points and etc. without any problems.

    Improved "Follow track" options in Navigation

    Click Navigation button in General screen -> Option -> Follow track. Here you can choose track for to follow or to import it from your device.

    Fixed issues with import/export of profile settings

    We fixed issues with import/export of profile settings for Custom package and Navigation profiles.
