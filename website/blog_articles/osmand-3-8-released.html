OsmAnd 3.8

September 10, 2020

Great news for Android users!

We're happy to announce the new 3.8 OsmAnd release for Android devices.

Update your OsmAnd, the new version has become even more convenient for your trips!

  • Updated "Plan Route" mode
  • Improved bicycle nodes visibility
  • Context menu for tracks with basic info
  • Improved "Search" algorithms
  • Improved "Follow track" options in Navigation
  • Fixed issues with import/export of profile settings
  • What else is in this release?
