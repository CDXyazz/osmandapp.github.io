OsmAnd Ski maps

July 15, 2015

OsmAnd Ski Maps is a plugin allowing you to navigate at winter sports locations. It provides you with a full map of ski pistes, ski lift markers and even colored lines indicating the complexity of each track.

Download the plugin, then use the menu item ‘﻿Configure Map’ to switch the style on.

How to use it

Download the plugin, then use the menu item ‘Configure Map’ to switch the winter and ski style on. Then find your desired area on the map and enjoy the info about the ski tracks including their complexity, location and infrastructure around. Besides that, you can see the nearby mountain peaks. The plugin also allows you to find the location of aerialway stations.
